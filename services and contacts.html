Shipping Vessels

Fataha commits to its price estimates, which means whatever Fantaha quotes you is what you’ll pay. So how does this work? Basically, every auto transport broker contracts from the same pool of car carriers. Fantaha will quote you a price that it thinks is fair to carriers, who in turn will bid on your shipment. If Fantaha can’t find a carrier to do the job at the estimated rate, we will still match you with a high-quality company—regardless of your budget—and pull up to $300 from its own pocket to subsidize the difference. Instead of having you pay a higher price than you expected or sending you a company with poor service, Fantaha will bend over backward to find you the best match—even if that means paying for part of your move. If this isn’t customer service done right, well, we don’t know what is.
